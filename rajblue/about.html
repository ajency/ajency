ABOUT


Rajah Blue Holdings Pte Ltd was founded in Singapore by a group of like-minded entrepreneurs. The Company manages a diverse range of investments across different geographies on behalf of the stakeholders, invests in seed-stage startups and operates an active business incubator from its shared service facility in Pasir Panjang.


Our stakeholders have all lived in Asia for most of their professional lives and have experience / interests in a wide range of industries and disciplines:



  • Mining Exploration

  • Software Development and Innovation

  • Banking and Finance

  • Telecommunications

  • Mobile Technologies

  • Real Estate

  • eCommerce

  • Mechanical Engineering

  • Food and Beverage

  • Media and Entertainment

  • Funds Management

  • Sales and Marketing

  • Retail and Wholesale Distribution

  • Commodity Trading and Agriculture
