Our process, here at UXboot

Run by experts and with well defined deliverables, our process ensures minimal involvement from you. So that’s one less thing on your plate to worry about.

01

Define

Defining the MVP Scope

It all starts with us discussing your idea, either through a phone call or a voice recording of your thoughts. We want to understand your point of view:

  • The target audience for your product
  • Their current pain points
  • How your product solves these problems
  • Your long term vision for the product
  • Any references etc.

Following this initial discussion, our UX director and Success Manager will put together a suggested MVP and service design. We will debate and discuss this with you, ending up with a finalized scope for your MVP and service design.

Deliverables
  • A finalised scope for the MVP
  • Service design

4 hrs

You put in

30 hrs

02

Design

Information Design

Following the defined MVP and service design blueprint, we will put together the information design - a list of data elements and their hierarchy. This would be discussed and debated with you, with a collaboration possible over Google docs and Hangouts. Our UX designer and Success Manager will then put together wireframes, using the information design as a base. These will be discussed with you on two separate phone calls. Collaboration with our copywriter will also be incorporated at this stage, to include crafted-copy.

Deliverables
  • Lo fidelity wireframes

10 hrs

You put in

100 hrs

We put in
03

Interaction

Visual and interaction Design

At this stage, we work on setting the design language for your MVP. We will create a mood board to portray our design concept, and define the colour palette, typography and primary interface for your MVP. We will then do a walkthrough of our design with you. When everything is agreed on, we will build the final clickable prototype, and walk it through with you.

Deliverables
  • Design Mockups
  • Clickable Prototypes
